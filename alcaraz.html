Carlos Alcaraz

Carlos Alcaraz Garfia is a Spanish professional tennis player who has quickly risen to fame as one of the brightest young stars in the sport. Born on May 5, 2003, in El Palmar, Murcia, Spain, Alcaraz is known for his powerful groundstrokes, athleticism, and mental toughness on the court.

Career Highlights

  • Youngest men's singles World No. 1 in ATP history
  • 2022 US Open Champion
  • 2023 Wimbledon Champion
  • Multiple ATP titles and Masters 1000 wins
  • Renowned for his electrifying playing style and sportsmanship

Playing Style

Alcaraz is celebrated for his aggressive baseline play, quick footwork, and ability to hit winners from both sides of the court. His all-court game and fearless attitude have made him a fan favorite worldwide.
